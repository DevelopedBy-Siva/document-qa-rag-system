Remote Work Policy - Version 1

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

1. OVERVIEW
This policy outlines the company's approach to remote work arrangements for all employees.

2. ELIGIBILITY
All full-time employees who have completed their probationary period (90 days) are eligible 
to request remote work arrangements.

3. REMOTE WORK DAYS
Employees are permitted to work remotely up to 2 days per week. Remote work days must be 
scheduled in advance with your manager and cannot be changed without prior approval.

4. CORE HOURS
All remote employees must be available during core business hours (9 AM - 5 PM EST) and 
respond to communications within 2 hours during these times.

5. EQUIPMENT
The company will provide necessary equipment including laptop, monitor, and headset. 
Employees are responsible for maintaining a suitable home office environment with reliable 
internet connectivity (minimum 25 Mbps download speed).

6. SECURITY
All company data must be accessed through VPN. Personal devices are not permitted to access 
company systems. Employees must ensure their home network is secured with WPA2 encryption.

7. PERFORMANCE
Remote work is a privilege that may be revoked if performance standards are not met. 
Managers will conduct monthly check-ins to ensure remote work arrangements are effective.

8. COMMUNICATION
Remote employees must attend all scheduled team meetings via video conference. Camera must 
be on during all meetings unless experiencing technical difficulties.

9. TERMINATION
This policy may be modified or terminated at any time at the company's discretion with 
30 days notice to affected employees.
