Remote Work Policy - Version 2

Effective Date: March 1, 2024

1. OVERVIEW
This policy outlines the company's updated approach to remote work arrangements for all employees.

2. ELIGIBILITY
All full-time employees who have completed their probationary period (60 days) are eligible 
to request remote work arrangements. Part-time employees working 30+ hours per week are also 
now eligible.

3. REMOTE WORK DAYS
Employees are permitted to work remotely up to 3 days per week. Remote work days must be 
scheduled in advance with your manager. Employees may request flexible scheduling with 
manager approval.

4. CORE HOURS
All remote employees must be available during core business hours (9 AM - 5 PM EST) and 
respond to communications within 2 hours during these times.

5. EQUIPMENT
The company will provide necessary equipment including laptop, monitor, headset, and 
ergonomic chair. Employees are responsible for maintaining a suitable home office environment 
with reliable internet connectivity (minimum 50 Mbps download speed). A $500 annual stipend 
is provided for home office improvements.

6. SECURITY
All company data must be accessed through VPN. Personal devices are not permitted to access 
company systems. Employees must ensure their home network is secured with WPA2 or WPA3 
encryption. Two-factor authentication is required for all remote access.

7. PERFORMANCE
Remote work is a privilege that may be revoked if performance standards are not met. 
Managers will conduct quarterly check-ins to ensure remote work arrangements are effective.

8. COMMUNICATION
Remote employees must attend all scheduled team meetings via video conference. Camera 
should be on during meetings when possible, but is not mandatory.

9. INTERNATIONAL REMOTE WORK
Employees may request to work remotely from international locations for up to 30 days per 
year with HR approval. Tax implications must be considered.

10. TERMINATION
This policy may be modified or terminated at any time at the company's discretion with 
30 days notice to affected employees.
